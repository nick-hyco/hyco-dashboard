Google Sheets connection
Paste your Apify output Google Sheet URL
1
In Apify, open your Instagram or TikTok scraper actor. Go to Output → Integrations → Google Sheets and connect your Google account.
2
Select or create a destination sheet. Apify will write scraped posts as rows after every run. Key columns to ensure are present: ownerUsername, type, likesCount, commentsCount, caption, timestamp, displayUrl.
3
In Google Sheets, click Share → Anyone with the link → set to Viewer. This lets the dashboard read it without requiring a login.
4
Copy the full sheet URL and paste it into the field above. Click Refresh feed — your posts will appear in the grid below.
5
After every Apify scrape run, just click Refresh feed again to pull the latest posts. One click, every time.
